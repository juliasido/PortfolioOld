Memories of Mal - Inception Movie

Memories of Mal is an interactive piece based on the movie Inception. Levels of Cobbs mind is represented by five vertical strips from reality to Limbo. The key on the left filters the location or Mal in the movie and the scroller on the right provides the time of your location in the timeline.
